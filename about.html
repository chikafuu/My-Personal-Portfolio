About Me

I'm someone who wears many hats — from cosplayer to call center warrior, gym-goer to gamer. I started working in the BPO industry back in 2022 as a student balancing work and school, and since then, I’ve journeyed through different companies, now proudly working in my 4th one. Each role taught me a little more about professionalism, resilience, and how far I can go when I stay consistent.

I’m an introvert at heart, but around close friends or trusted colleagues, I definitely have my extrovert moments. I value personal space and quiet time — especially when my social battery runs low. Cleanliness is a must for me (taking a shower after being outside is nonnegotiable — always!).

In my free time, you’ll find me doing something creative or cathartic: cosplaying characters I love, getting lost in video games, playing the flute, hitting the gym or going for a bike ride, or journaling to process my thoughts and experiences.
